Our Purpose

Because Compare.com was founded on the idea that shopping for car insurance should be an experience that’s quick, easy, informative – and even enjoyable – we promise our customers that we’ll never stray from this core belief. We’ve worked hard to create a user-friendly site experience that makes shopping for an auto insurance policy as easy as a Sunday drive.

We started Compare.com in 2013 to make saving money on car insurance easy. And while we currently help our customers find solutions for more than just car insurance, our purpose is the same — to save you money.

How We Make Money

Since Compare.com is 100% free to use, you might be wondering how we keep the lights on. The answer is simple: We make money by saving you money.

That means most of our revenue comes through commissions from our carrier partners when you purchase a policy from one of the quotes you see on our site. We don’t sell your information to fund our services, like some other sites.
